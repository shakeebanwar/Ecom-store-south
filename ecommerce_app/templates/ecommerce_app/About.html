The NileBrand

(The) Nile Brand service is focused on providing products from Africans that are both based in the USA and in African countries. Their goal is not to just provide products but to educate people on Africa and its history. We are coming to a critical moment as the generations grow they are likely to have less and less knowledge of their roots, where they come from and what the culture of their parents and their parents meant.

We Help Empower ROSS

The Nile Brand celebrates and embraces the unique purpose of all South Sudanese. The world's youngest nation reflects the shared aspirations of millions of South Sudanese to move past decades of war and build a peaceful, equitable country. This Event was founded in June 2021 as a self-development platform devoted to celebrating South Sudan the development, women empowerment and South Sudan Independence. The South Sudanese Independence Celebration and Network Soiree initially began as a one day event with over 20 people in attendance. Due to the presentation of curated topics, an engaging array and networking opportunities, it now spans 2 days with a seminar and will host many leaders from across the country.

Networking & Entrepreneurship

The South Sudanese Independence Celebration and Network Soiree embraces and enables entrepreneurship alongside community support.
